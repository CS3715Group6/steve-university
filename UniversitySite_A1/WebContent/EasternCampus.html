Welcome to the Eastern Campus Located in St. John's Newfoundland

The Eastern Campus is located roughly in the center of St. John's. The campus has approximabley twenty thousand students. The campus includes building and facilites for various departments and also housing for students. If students prefer to live off campus St. John's has various methods of transportation for commuting.

The campus contains seven main buildings listed below with building codes to the right

  • Administration - Building HH
  • Mathematics - Building MS
  • Computer Science - Building SC
  • Education - Building MP
  • Geology - Building TH
  • Business - Building MH
  • Library - Building LC
